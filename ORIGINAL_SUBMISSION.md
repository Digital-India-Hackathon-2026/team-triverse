## Idea
Many patients need costly MRI scans because X-rays lack soft tissue detail. Our AI generates MRI-like images from X-rays to assist diagnosis, reduce unnecessary MRI referrals, lower costs, and improve access to advanced imaging, especially in resource-limited areas.
